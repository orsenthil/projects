About Me

Technical professional with a passion for innovation

I'm a passionate technologist with over 20 years of experience spanning the entire software development ecosystem. From core Python development to cutting-edge AI applications, from cryptocurrency mining to cultural preservation, my journey reflects a deep commitment to both technical excellence and social impact.

Core Contributions: As a CPython core developer, I've contributed to Python's urllib and urlparse modules, and played a crucial role in CPython's historic migration from Mercurial to Git. My work has directly impacted millions of Python developers worldwide.

Educational Impact: Through LearnToSolveIt.com, I've been teaching C programming for two decades, helping countless students master fundamental concepts. My educational projects span from elementary math quizzes to comprehensive course documentation for Georgia Tech OMSCS students.

Community Building: I founded the Sourashtra Association and Thinnal.org, fostering a community of 1000+ members dedicated to language preservation. From traditional recipe collections to cultural education programs, I believe technology should serve humanity's diverse heritage.
